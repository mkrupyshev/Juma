Booming Games

As a software developer, Booming Games is the creative branch of the Juma Group.

They are a leading B2B provider of proprietary content for the gaming industry, combining excellent gaming experiences for players with enterprising business for partnering operators and resellers.

From uniquely novel configurations and interfaces, to highly entertaining themes and characters, the Booming Games ever-growing catalogue consists of over 50 games, all of which are crafted to be fun, engaging, and will keep players coming back for more.

Entergaming Platform

Entergaming is Juma Group’s proprietary state-of-the-art platform service which grew out of over 10 years of proven industry success.

Built with a modular approach, Entergaming services the retail betting and gaming industries.

The core components powering Entergaming’s services include:

Multi-Sales Channel Platform
All-in-One Back Office
Built-In Sports Betting Engine
Central Risk Management
Proprietary Bonus Engine
Multiple Language, Currency And Time-Zone Support Affiliate And Multilevel Tree System
